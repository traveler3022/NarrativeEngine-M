Monarchy - **Capital:** Valdris (stone & banner) - **Ruler:** King

vying for favor - **Great Houses:** Valerian (Royal), Medici (Bank),

demi-humans:** second-class citizens or "exotic labor" \###

Dominion --- Empire of the Dawn - Type: Imperial Magocracy -
**Capital:** Sakurajima (volcanic spire) - **Ruler:** Empress Long Yexin
(dragon-blood) - **Structure:** Five Great Clans balancing military and
spiritual duties - **Great Clans:** Tang (Poison), Murong (War), Ye
(Shadow), Zhuge (Strategy), Long (Royal) - **Logic:** honor, "Face,"
cultivation rank - **Attitude to others:** isolationist superiority \###
WL-FACTION-THE-ARGENT-CONFEDERACY-DEMI-HUMAN-MERCHANT-REPUB Title: The
Argent Confederacy --- Demi-Human Merchant Republic - Type: Federated
Republic - **Capital:** Harmony Spire (crystal-tech coastal
metropolis) - **Ruler:** Council of Species (elected speakers) -
**Population:** Beastkin (lion/wolf-folk), civilized orcs, exiled elves,
tieflings - **Structure:** city-states united by trade and defense
against human expansion - **Vibe:** Renaissance Venice meets High
Fantasy; inventive, socially chaotic, fiercely free - **Logic:** "Your
blood doesn't matter; your contribution does." - **Strength:** naval
superiority + magitech artificers \### WL-FACTION-THE-BORDER-MARCHES
