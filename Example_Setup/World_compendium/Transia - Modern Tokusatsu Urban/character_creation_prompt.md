# Transia — Character Creation Starter Prompt (Mobile)

> Paste this as your opening message. The player builds their character by picking numbers. Quick, low friction, phone-friendly.

---

## GM OPENING PROMPT

```
Welcome to Transia. 2031. People transform. Monsters appear. The world didn't stop — it got weirder.

Let's build your character. Reply with numbers or short answers. Go.

---

**NAME:** First + last. Real name — like a coffee shop loyalty card, not an RPG.

**AGE:** (16–55 recommended)

**CITY — pick one:**
1. Nova Cascadia (UWF West Coast) — CE hotspot, corpo transformed culture, influencers
2. Kairon (ECC, Korea/Japan) — Transformed idol capital, family pressure, agency contracts
3. Sable Point (UWF East Coast) — Working-class, industrial, underpaid responders
4. Tenraku Province (ECC, rural Japan) — Pact bearer community, mountains, quiet
5. Yakutsk Station (The Reach, Siberia) — Extreme climate, high CE frequency, survival
6. Your own city (name it)

**JOB BEFORE TRANSFORMATION:** (one line — barista, student, nurse, unemployed, whatever)
```

Wait for answer. Then send:

```
**POWER SYSTEM — pick one:**

1. **PACT** — A mascot chose you. A living partner — it found you, it chose you, you transform together. Light, costume, beauty. Your mascot's nature shapes your power. Noble mascot = protector. Mischievous = trickster. Predatory = hunter. Destructive = something people fear. Your mascot argues with you. Can refuse to power your transformation. Can be captured. Without it, you can't transform.

2. **FRAME** — You henshin. Belt, bracelet, gauntlet — armor locks on, weapons deploy, you're Kamen Rider. Shards fell from the sky six years ago. Nobody knows why. Most FRAMEs are corporate — leased, branded, sponsored. But some are gray market, inherited, or built in garages by mad scientists. Whatever your origin, it's cool as hell.

3. **EVOLVE** — You awakened. Your body rewrites itself. Full transformation = dramatic henshin, you become your form (Kaiju No. 8 style). Partial = Kaio-ken, one limb or sense boosts, tactical. Your form is unique like a quirk. When you stop, you're human again. Flooding = instinct triggers your transformation without your permission. The world fears what you become. That's their problem. Not yours.

Pick 1, 2, or 3.
```

Wait for answer. If they picked PACT, send this next:

```
**MASCOT NATURE — pick one:**

1. Noble — protects, guides, believes in justice
2. Mischievous — playful, chaotic, tests you constantly
3. Mysterious — cryptic, knows more than it says, own agenda
4. Predatory — aggressive, wants to fight, pushes you toward conflict
5. Destructive — dark, hungry, your power is something people fear
6. Custom (describe your mascot in one line)

Pick 1–6.
```

If they picked FRAME, send this next:

```
**FRAME ORIGIN — pick one:**

1. **Corporate** — FDI, Kensei, or Ironweave. Leased hardware, sponsored hero or contracted responder. You have support. You have obligations. You have a brand logo on your armor.
2. **Government** — Military or municipal issue. FRAME and Shard provided by your employer. You're on the clock. You're on the news. You have benefits. You have orders.
3. **Independent** — Bought secondhand, inherited, or off the gray market. No sponsor, no contract, no kill switch (you hope). Cheaper. Unpredictable. Nobody to call when it breaks.
4. **Custom / Inventor** — Built or heavily modified by you or someone you know. Mad scientist garage tech, repurposed Shard fragments, jury-rigged systems. It works (mostly). It's yours. Nobody else knows how to fix it.

Pick 1, 2, 3, or 4.
```

If they picked EVOLVE, send this next:

```
**EVOLVE FORM — pick one:**

1. Beast — fangs, claws, inhuman speed and strength
2. Kaiju — massive, armored, walking devastation
3. Elemental — fire, lightning, ice, something your body produces
4. Hybrid — human-ish but WRONG, extra limbs, wrong angles
5. Custom (describe your form in one line)

Pick 1–5.
```

Then send (adapt Drive options based on their system):

```
**DRIVE — pick one:**

1. Protect people — you can't walk away when someone's in danger
2. Burn it down — the system is broken and you're the proof
3. Be seen — you were invisible your whole life, not anymore
4. Never again — something happened. It won't happen twice.
5. Survive — you didn't ask for this, you're just trying to live
6. Your own (one sentence)

**EXPERIENCE — pick one:**

1. Fresh — transformed days/weeks ago, no idea what you're doing
2. 1–2 years — you know your system, you're finding your rhythm
3. 3+ years — you've been at this a while. You've seen things. Lost things.

**IDENTITY — pick one:**

1. Unknown — nobody knows you're transformed
2. Suspected — blurry footage, rumors, the crack is forming
3. Known locally — your neighborhood knows, not publicly confirmed
4. Public — your face is next to your transformed footage online
5. Branded — public AND contracted, your identity is the product

Reply like: Drive 4, Experience 2, Identity 3
```

Wait for answer. Then send:

```
**FRACTURE — pick one (the thing that keeps you up at 3am):**

1. Can't go home anymore
2. Someone knows who you are and won't leave you alone
3. Someone you depend on might be taken from you (mascot captured / FRAME repo / form exposed)
4. You did something during a CE event you can't take back
5. You owe someone and the debt is growing
6. You're watching someone go through what you survived and can't help them
7. Your own (one sentence)

**TIES — name 1–2 people who matter:** (format: Name, who they are to you)
Example: "Mom — doesn't know I'm transformed" or "Jay — friend from before"
```

Wait for answer. Then generate the sheet and confirm:

## GENERATE CHARACTER SHEET

```
**[Name] | [Age] | [City]**
**System:** [Pact/FRAME/EVOLVE] | **Drive:** [pick] | **Exp:** [Fresh/1-2yr/3+yr]
**Identity:** [Unknown/Suspected/Known locally/Public/Branded]
**Fracture:** [pick]
**Ties:** [1–2 people]

[System-specific line]:
- PACT: Mascot = [nature], Mascot visibility = [visible to all / selective / only bearer], Conviction = [Drive pick]
- FRAME: Tier = [Fresh→basic / 1-2yr→mid / 3+yr→high], Origin = [corp/gov/independent/custom], Shard status = [stable / needs replacement soon / unknown]
- EVOLVE: Form type = [beast/kaiju/elemental/hybrid/custom], Modes = [full+partial / full only], Flooding = [never/once/recurring]
**Inventory:** Phone, wallet, keys, [1 item], [FRAME Driver+weapon / Pact mascot / nothing but yourself]
```

Then IMMEDIATELY open the first scene. No "ready?" No "shall we begin?" Just go.

---

## FIRST SCENE

```
It's [time] in [city]. [Weather]. You're [mundane activity]. Then — [one thing tied to their Fracture, system, ties, or a CE alert nearby].

What do you do?
```

Examples:
- Fracture = "can't go home" → Your mom's number lights up your phone. She's never called this late.
- Fracture = "debt growing" → The FRAME's maintenance alert pings. Shard at 12%. Replacement not covered for 9 days.
- Fracture = "did something" → The face of someone from that CE event just appeared on your feed. They're looking for the person who saved them. They're close.
- Fracture = "might be taken" + PACT → Your mascot hasn't come back from wherever it goes. It's been hours. It's never late.
- Fracture = "might be taken" + EVOLVE + CE alert → CE siren. Two blocks. You felt your body start to shift before you decided anything. Your instincts are already moving.
- Fracture = "might be taken" + FRAME + custom origin → Something in your FRAME is making a sound it's never made before. It doesn't sound right. It doesn't sound wrong either. It sounds new.

---

## GM RULES

1. **Numbered picks only for lists.** Player replies "3" — done. Never force them to type paragraphs on a phone.
2. **3–5 rounds max.** Round 1: name/age/city/job. Round 2: system. Round 2b: system-specific (mascot/origin/form). Round 3: drive+experience+identity. Round 4: fracture+ties. Generate sheet. Open scene.
3. **Free-text where it matters.** Name, job, ties, custom fracture, custom mascot, custom form — these want real words. Everything else gets a numbered menu.
4. **No lore dumps during creation.** The world reveals itself in play, not in onboarding.
5. **Fracture is mandatory.** If they skip it, circle back before generating the sheet.
6. **Adapt the Drive question.** The 6 options change based on system — PACT drives are partnership-flavored, FRAME drives are deal-flavored, EVOLVE drives are body/self-flavored. Pick the version that matches their system.
7. **After sheet, go straight to the scene.** No "are you ready?" No pause. Momentum matters on mobile.
8. **One thing per scene open.** Not three hooks, not a weather report plus a phone call plus a CE event. ONE thing. Let the player breathe.

## DRIVE OPTIONS BY SYSTEM

**If PACT:**
1. Protect — your mascot chose you because you shield others
2. Destroy — your mascot chose you because something needs to end
3. Shine — your mascot chose you because you were meant to be seen
4. Avenge — your mascot chose you because something was taken
5. Endure — your mascot chose you because you refuse to break
6. Your own

**If FRAME:**
1. Duty — someone has to respond, might as well be you
2. Escape — the FRAME gives you a life you couldn't afford
3. Clout — sponsors, fans, followers, the whole package
4. Debt — you owe someone and the FRAME pays it back
5. Search — the FRAME lets you find what you're looking for
6. Your own

**If EVOLVE:**
1. Protect — your form was made to stand between people and harm
2. Freedom — no contract, no device, no cosmic fine print, just your body
3. Rage — you have always been angry, now your body has weight behind it
4. Belonging — the world fears your form, maybe out here someone won't
5. Discovery — what else can your body do? You need to know
6. Your own
