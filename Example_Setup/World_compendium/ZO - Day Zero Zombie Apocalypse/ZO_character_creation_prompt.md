# ZO — Character Creation

Answer each question with the number. No essays. We'll build your survivor step by step.

---

## Step 1 — Name

What's your character called?

1. Japanese name
2. Nickname/callsign only (real name unknown)
3. Half — Japanese name, but everyone calls them by a nickname

Pick 1, 2, or 3. If 1 or 3, type the name after the number.

---

## Step 2 — Age & Build

Choose one from each:

**Age:**
1. Young (16-22) — still growing into yourself
2. Prime (23-35) — physical peak
3. Experienced (36-50) — been through things
4. Old guard (51+) — body's slowing, mind isn't

**Build:**
1. Lean — fast, wiry, not much padding
2. Average — nothing special, nothing weak
3. Athletic — trained, capable, noticeable
4. Heavy — strong, slow, durable

---

## Step 3 — What were you before Day Zero?

Pick one:
1. Office worker (salaryman, admin, corporate)
2. Student (high school or university)
3. Service worker (retail, food, delivery, transport)
4. Skilled trade (construction, mechanic, cooking, medical, engineering)
5. Emergency/civil service (police, fire, coast guard, medical staff)
6. Criminal/underground (yakuza, scammer, thief, enforcer)
7. Homemaker / retired / unemployed
8. Other — type it

---

## Step 4 — Where were you at 06:47?

The outbreak started. Where were you?
1. On a train or in a station
2. At work or school
3. At home or in residential area
4. Outdoors — commuting, running, walking
5. Somewhere you shouldn't have been
6. You choose for me

---

## Step 5 — One thing you grabbed before running

Pick one:
1. Weapon (knife, bat, tool — something you could hit with)
2. Phone (dead in hours, but you tried)
3. Food or water
4. A keepsake (photo, charm, something personal)
5. Nothing — you just ran
6. Something weird — type it

---

## Step 6 — What matters most to you now?

Pick one:
1. Staying alive, period
2. Finding someone (family, friend, partner)
3. Protecting the people around me
4. Understanding what happened
5. Getting strong enough that nothing can hurt me again
6. Revenge — someone or something is going to pay

---

## Step 7 — Awakening

You absorbed a crystal. It happened. Now you have power.

**First — your category:**
1. Enhancement (strength, speed, durability, reflexes, senses)
2. Elemental (fire, ice, electricity, wind, vibration, light)
3. Utility (perception, environment manipulation, pathfinding, structural sense)
4. Affinity (empathic sensing, animal bonding, infection sensing, group coordination)
5. Hybrid (two weak categories — type which two)
6. Surprise me

**Second — your specific power within that category:**
Type it, or pick 6 and I'll assign one.

(Examples: Enhancement → wall-running speed. Elemental → ice. Utility → can sense structural weak points. Affinity → can tell if someone is lying.)

**Third — your material requirement:**
You need a specific thing to use your power. No material = no power. This was random. You didn't choose it.

1. Something common in a megacity (duct tape, batteries, coins, tissues)
2. Something specific and annoying (rubber bands, super glue, vinegar, candles)
3. Something food-related (spinach, instant coffee, soy sauce, sugar packets)
4. Something electronic (USB cables, earbuds, watch batteries, sim cards)
5. Surprise me — I'll assign something weird

---

## Step 8 — Your starting tier

Everyone starts Tier 1. You just awakened. You barely know how to use your power, you need your material every time, and it drains you fast.

Confirm: **Ready to start at Tier 1?**
1. Yes
2. No — I have a backstory reason for starting higher (type it, GM approves)

---

## Steps Complete

When you've answered all steps, send your answers. The GM will generate your character sheet from the world lore.

Format example:
```
Step 1: 3 — Tanaka Riku, everyone calls him "Rik"
Step 2: 2, 1 — Prime age, lean build
Step 3: 3 — convenience store clerk
Step 4: 1 — on the Yamanote line
Step 5: 1 — box cutter from behind the counter
Step 6: 2 — finding my younger sister
Step 7: 1, wall-jumping speed, 3 — needs instant coffee powder
Step 8: 1
```

That's it. Go.
