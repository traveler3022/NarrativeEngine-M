## Critical Risk: No Tests

The biggest risk is not architectural — it's the complete absence
of test coverage. Any refactoring is blind.

**Recommendation:** Before Phase 3 (Implementation), add at minimum:
- Smoke test: app loads without crash
- Smoke test: send message → AI responds
- Smoke test: campaign switch works
- Smoke test: NPC add works
