### 4. Is the design minimal?

- 6 ports (not 10 — 2 rejected with evidence)
- 35 methods (not 60+ — 1 removed for being speculative)
- 6 adapters (one per port, no extras)
- 1 persistence service (not a port — YAGNI)
- 0 event buses (no evidence supports need)

**Design is minimal.** ✅

### 5. Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Logic extraction from store (22 violations) may break runtime | Add smoke tests first |
| God File splits may introduce circular deps | Extract shared helpers first |

## Verdict: ✅ Design is sound, evidence-backed, and minimal.
